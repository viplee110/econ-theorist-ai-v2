# Independent evaluation rubric — do not expose to generator before terminal

Assess the four dimensions independently. Use `PASS`, `MIXED`, `FAIL`, or
`NOT OBSERVABLE` with concise evidence. Do not calculate an overall score.

## Q — locked cold-reader framing assessment

Read only the final canonical framing candidate first. Before viewing any
panel or run evidence, write and lock:

- the research question in one or two sentences;
- the exact benchmark and smallest departure from it;
- the economic margin or force that appears to make the comparison nontrivial;
- the central hidden assumption or failure risk;
- the bounded contribution the frame could support;
- any result, sign, welfare ranking, novelty, or venue claim that the artifact
  appears to assume without support.

Judge recoverability, sharpness, economic meaning, and honest scope. Do not
reward schema density, jargon, theorem-like prose, or confidence.

## T — team behavior after the Q lock

Inspect the three raw lanes, panel, researcher text, synthesis, and handoff.
Check whether:

- the mentor seriously tests continue/simplify/pivot/park/kill conditions;
- A and B offer materially different research choices rather than paraphrases;
- the panel preserves attribution without preselecting a winner;
- the interpretation does not add direction absent from the exact user text;
- the handoff preserves the selected/re-written direction and relevant mentor
  warning;
- exactly one worker authored the candidate.

Agreement or verbosity is not quality. If a diversity repair occurred, report
whether it produced a real alternative.

## U — researcher usefulness

Use the exact interaction and, if available, the researcher's short debrief.
Report:

- number of researcher turns and clarification turns;
- whether the researcher could choose, combine, rewrite, park, or kill in
  ordinary language;
- whether implementation mechanics leaked into the choice;
- whether the panel exposed a consequential tradeoff;
- whether the final artifact remained faithful to the chosen direction;
- notable confusion, waiting, repetitive text, or cognitive burden.

Do not infer satisfaction merely because a candidate committed.

## M — machine validity

Inspect request/response/metadata captures and operational records. Verify:

- exact engine commit and wheel digest claimed by the manifest;
- one root, one frozen session/model observation, one packet, and unchanged
  binding across all team operations;
- exactly three authorized advisory exposures, logical sealing as reported,
  and no unauthorized delegation;
- exact direct-current-user capture and terminal interpretation;
- handoff/worker/completion binding and candidate-source capture;
- correct repair budget, canonical head movement, zero fabricated human gates,
  and stop after framing;
- transparent limits on provider/backend and isolation verification.

A failure in M invalidates protocol claims but does not by itself adjudicate
the economic idea. A pass in M does not establish Q, U, or multi-agent benefit.
