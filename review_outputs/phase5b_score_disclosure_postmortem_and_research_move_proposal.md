## Why G1--G3 approval and G4 denial are consistent

G1--G3 purchased information; G4 decided whether the information justified a
paper investment.

- **G1:** the source-isolated
  [`nondegeneracy probe`](phase5b0_framing_team_public_pilot_followup/probe/NONDEGENERACY_PROBE.md)
  supplied an illustrative `phi(s,r)=r` witness with strict margins and
  continuity. After the researcher separately approved that rule, the witness
  became applicable to the maintained model and established a nonempty open
  crossing region. Replacing the unsupported strict `frontier` with an
  outcome-vector locus also made the question honest. This justified
  exploration without claiming a contribution.
- **G2:** the middle-score entry mechanism was falsifiable, and the low-score
  exit mechanism remained a serious opposite-direction rival. Mixed and
  no-crossing regions, ablations, and the finite-example limitation were
  preserved.
- **G3:** the separable likelihood-ratio implementation supplied a transparent
  common cutoff and low-dimensional representation. Formal promotion was
  needed to determine whether that representation supported a portable
  theorem or merely compressed an application.
- **G4:** all final R3 obligations were verified, but the closest-theory and
  absorption objects found a standard Bayesian posterior-cutoff plus costly
  self-selection core. The first located mapping failure was the
  application-specific post-rejection timing. The retained result therefore
  consists of conditional entry and exit regions, exact outcome-vector flows,
  and a no-unconditional-ranking boundary rather than a new general theory.

The system was right to preserve this formally valid application result and
right to stop before prose production.

## What should move earlier

The full G4 novelty and investment decision must not be moved to G1. General
structure sometimes appears only after proof, and an early hard
“looks standard” rule would kill legitimate discovery.

The lower-cost signals should move earlier:

1. Before G1, retain the existing nondegeneracy, strict-margin, and
   beyond-enumeration checks, and explore rivals when they are naturally
   available. Require one serious rival by G2, where the theory kernel owns
   that obligation.
2. At the G2-to-G3 investment point, surface a non-promoting
   contribution-radius warning inside the compatible engine-owned WorkPacket
   context. Strip away application nouns and ask whether the expected chain is
   more than a known update, threshold, and accounting argument.
3. At the same point, forecast the first closest-theory mapping failure. If it
   is only timing, labels, or an application object, report
   `application-only risk high`; do not assert novelty and do not auto-kill.
4. Surface `distinctive_mechanism_contribution_status=not_claimed` beside the
   G3 investment choice. It is a warning about proof budget, not a gate
   outcome.

Exploratory counterexamples, ablations, and assumption-role probes remain
early G2/G3 work. The late strict checks remain exhaustive claim-bound
proof/interpretation verification, formal literature/novelty absorption,
`ResultPortfolio` curation, G4, and all authoring work.

## Proposed development cards

These cards are advisory search transformations. They may add evidence to
existing scientific objects, but they never own navigation, emit a route
disposition, create a WorkPacket, or replace a human gate.

### 1. Computational Structure Probe

**Trigger.** The smallest model looks like finite-cell arithmetic; existence
of a nonempty open effect region is unclear; or an unresolved rule or primitive
may make the model degenerate.

**Required semantic inputs.** Exact question, benchmark delta, candidate
primitive/mechanism graph, parameter domain, fixed decision rule, tie
convention, and kill condition. A missing human-owned primitive choice is
reported as an unresolved dependency and stops the move; only the normal
engine route and human authority can decide its disposition.

**Operation.** Solve bounded finite or numerical cases to find strict
crossings, perturbation margins, counterexamples, and a candidate cutoff,
sufficient statistic, equivalence, or functional form. Pair a numerical
witness with an analytic continuity or proof obligation when claiming a
nonempty open region.

**Existing-object output.** `ExampleSuite`, `PredictionRegister`, and a tested
domain; any general statement remains a conjecture for later `ClaimGraph` and
`ProofObligation` work.

**Advisory evidence.**

- a strict witness plus an interpretable structural conjecture emits
  `supports_continuation` evidence for the normal route or gate;
- a missing researcher-owned primitive is surfaced without filling it in;
- an analytic proof of no admissible open region gives the normal route or
  gate evidence to consider killing that mechanism, not automatically the
  whole question; and
- failed numerical search alone: no nonexistence claim.

**Candidate source families for audit.** Varian's explicit simple-case method,
Annie Liang's explicit predictive/adversarial model-probe method, and an
independent paper reconstruction that actively searches for intuitive
comparative-static failures. No source is admitted by this proposal alone.

### 2. Representation Hunter

**Trigger.** A cold reader can recover only cell-by-cell thresholds; primitive
ordering, a low-dimensional statistic, or a transferable mechanism is absent.

**Required semantic inputs.** Exact benchmark, mechanism chain, the probe's
witnesses and counterexamples, and the current formal candidate if one exists.

**Operation.** Restate the problem through beliefs, likelihood ratios,
cutoffs, continuation values, sufficient statistics, dual variables, or
another natural representation. Record the first place where compression
fails.

**Existing-object output.** A rival `MechanismHypothesis`,
`FormalizationMap`, or scoped `ClaimGraph` boundary. In this case the useful
diagnostic representation was the likelihood-ratio product and the pooled
score aggregates; their existence did not itself establish novelty.

**Advisory evidence.**

- a representation that reveals a robust ordering, decomposition, or nearby
  transfer emits `supports_continuation` evidence;
- a notation-only compression with no new economic job emits
  `application_only_risk`;
- loss of load-bearing institutional detail: reject only the representation,
  not the question.

**Candidate source families for audit.** Independent published-paper
reconstructions from posterior, cutoff-price, representation-change, and
continuation-state traditions. They must be labeled
`inferred_reconstruction` with published-outcome bias, not attributed as an
author's private discovery process.

### 3. Analogical Structure Transfer — First Mapping Failure

**Trigger.** Collaborator proposals retain the same mechanism under different
labels; the proposed contribution may be a standard structure embedded in a
new application; or the contribution radius is unclear before large proof
investment.

**Required semantic inputs.** Exact benchmark delta, a
primitive-to-response-to-outcome mechanism chain, any useful representation
map, and one function-only comparator structure.

**Operation.** Map primitives and strategic arrows to a genuinely different
setting. Preserve the first failed mapping rather than shared vocabulary. Ask
whether that failure changes a prediction, mechanism, feasible object, or
economic consequence.

**Existing-object output.** A genuinely rival `MechanismHypothesis`, a new
boundary, or a non-promoting absorption-risk warning. Normal mechanism
tournament, economic-argument, and closest-theory objects remain the owners of
scientific output.

**Advisory evidence.**

- complete structural isomorphism with no new consequence emits
  `application_only_risk`;
- a mapping failure that changes the mechanism or prediction emits
  `supports_continuation`;
- a required capacity, congestion, endogenous institution, or different
  research object stops the move and reports that a separately human-approved
  reframe would be required; the move does not emit or own
  `new_brief_required`; and
- analogy alone: never a novelty verdict.

**Candidate source families for audit.** Independent paper reconstructions
that unify definitions, isolate belief primitives, or compress portable
externality structures, plus an explicit skeptical source about fables and
mapping. Conflicting evidence must remain visible.

## Why other moves are not in the first batch

- A new “nondegeneracy check” card would merely duplicate an existing
  scientific floor.
- `Robustness-Axis Switch` could wrongly make broad robustness a universal
  quality requirement for institution-specific theory.
- `Institutional Feedback Deepener` and `Nonconvex Branch Mutation` can be
  valuable later, but using them now would look like an attempt to rescue the
  closed case by adding capacity, congestion, or endogeneity.
- `Different-Implementation Question` remains a candidate for a later batch;
  the three selected moves more directly address the observed finite-cell,
  representation, and absorption failures.
- The setup-burden diagnostic remains an evaluator measure, not a
  `ResearchMove`.
