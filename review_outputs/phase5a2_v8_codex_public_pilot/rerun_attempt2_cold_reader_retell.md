# Cold-reader retell

## 1. Research question

The question is whether a certificate that is publicly visible but used up in
trade can affect buyers through the certificate stock itself. More precisely,
the researcher needs to show what would have to be true before a buyer-side
comparative response could be attributed to that stock.

## 2. What a certificate appears to guarantee

The memo does not state what the certificate certifies or what substantive
property it guarantees. It establishes only that certification has a cost and
that the certificate is public and consumed in trade. Any stronger guarantee
would require guessing.

## 3. Buyer's apparent actions when a certificate is visible

No concrete buyer action is specified. The memo refers to a possible buyer-side
comparative response and to a choice tradeoff, but says that the operative
payoff comparison is unresolved. Thus the buyer's action set, choice rule, and
the direction of any response cannot be recovered.

## 4. Missing causal link and why it matters

The missing link is an active choice tradeoff: the graph does not show an
operative payoff comparison that connects the certificate/stock state to a
buyer choice. Without that active-margin witness, the proposed stock channel
remains conditional and does not establish that buyer behavior responds to
stock composition. Consequently, no buyer-side comparative result or
mechanism attribution is justified.

## 5. What the researcher must do next, and what would kill the branch

The researcher must repair the upstream primitive graph so that it supports an
exact active-margin witness and attribution tied to the stated benchmarks. The
proposed branch would fail if that repair cannot produce a witnessed choice
tradeoff and a resolved operative payoff comparison; in that event it cannot
support a mechanism contribution, G1 readiness, or a welfare conclusion.

## 6. Points not recoverable from the memo without guessing

- What the certificate certifies or guarantees.
- The buyer's available actions, decision timing, and choice rule.
- The sellers' concrete actions and the buyer's payoff or utility function.
- The exact meaning of stock composition and how it enters the buyer's
  information or payoff state.
- The precise transition rule linking certification, carryover or consumption,
  and stock.
- The operative payoff comparison and the exact active margin it would define.
- Any directional comparative result, equilibrium outcome, welfare effect, or
  quantitative result.
- The values or row-by-row findings of the frozen-policy,
  no-carryover, and inspection-disabled benchmarks.
- How the proposed information-state transition changes behavior in any
  particular history or date.
- The exact equilibrium equations and how the proposed stationary
  Markov-perfect equilibria are obtained.
- Whether the common tie-breaking and selection convention has been applied
  to a completed model or is only a proposed scope condition.

## 7. Provisional reconstruction-burden label: H3

The memo gives a coherent diagnosis and a bounded repair target: the research
question, proposed stock channel, and missing active-margin witness are
recoverable. However, the certificate's substantive meaning, buyer action, and
operative payoff comparison are not specified, and the central causal result
is explicitly not established. Reconstructing a usable model and an attributed
result therefore requires substantial but focused upstream reconstruction,
rather than a merely cosmetic clarification or a wholly unrecoverable
proposal.
