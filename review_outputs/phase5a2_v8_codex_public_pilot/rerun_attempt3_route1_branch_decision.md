# R3 guaranteed-service branch decision

Status: **researcher choice frozen before generation; not a G1 Decision**

On 2026-07-18 (Asia/Shanghai), after receiving a comparison of the three
economically admissible branches in the independent R2 evaluation, the user

guaranteed-service certificate ledger.

## Scientific interpretation

- A truthful certificate binds one particular high service opportunity.
- While the opportunity and certificate are carried, certified trade succeeds
  and gives the buyer normalized value one under common terms.
- The opportunity and certificate are consumed only when that certified trade
  occurs.
- This choice does not pre-approve the timing of later fresh draws or the law
  of motion for uncertified opportunities. R3 must propose those objects
  explicitly, and they remain pre-G1 scientific proposals.
- When at least one certified opportunity is selectable, inspecting an
  uncertified opportunity is strictly dominated for `c > 0` and at best tied
  at `c = 0` absent another buyer-facing force.
- The case must therefore kill the unsupported positive-stock inspection
  margin. It may study zero-certificate-state inspection, state-distribution
  reweighting, allocation, or buyer surplus only to the extent that their
  state, action, payoff, and transition links can be stated honestly.
- Price, match value, congestion, rationing, or another buyer-facing force may
  not be added merely to rescue positive-stock inspection. Such a new primitive
  would require a separate human scientific choice.
