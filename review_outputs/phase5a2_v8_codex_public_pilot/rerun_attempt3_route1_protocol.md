## Execution and stop rule

Use `capture_codex_invocation.py` for every bridge call, passing the exact
installed `.venv\Scripts\etai.exe` launcher. Every invocation receives fresh
request/stdout/stderr/metadata names under `run/`. Every source-reading
completion also supplies the packet-declared candidate path via
`--candidate-source`.

A capture binding error, source mutation, digest mismatch, or invalid bridge
response is a transport/evidence failure and stops the task. For a valid
candidate rejection, use at most the engine-declared two sequential repairs.
Stop at the first human decision boundary, natural protocol stop, or exhausted
repair budget. A real post-delivery terminal failure may be recorded with the
exact bridge `finish` contract; an ordinary human wait is not a finish.

The final generator report must bind every route claim to its request,
response, capture metadata, candidate snapshot, engine commit, wheel digest,
canonical head, and observable model label. Provider/backend identity remains
unconfirmed unless independently exposed by the host.
