  engine, network, subagent, directory-external scientific input, or canonical

- The route candidates in this follow-up were developer-assisted and
  nonblind. They are not fresh WorkPacket-only model evidence and do not prove

  the rejected diagnostic attempts are not represented as canonical pilot

  `user-selected-current-model-unverified`. The provider/backend identity was

  Git HEAD was
  `3774d0a3698e61ebfb237d222441bff95b715f9b`. No exact wheel digest or frozen

- Crash-recovery and exact-retry code hardening performed after these captures

## Inventory and verification

`evidence_manifest.json` inventories every evidence file in this sibling
archive except itself and its sidecar. `evidence_manifest.sha256` contains the
SHA-256 of the manifest. The manifest declares those two structural exclusions
and the exact expected directory membership, so an extra, missing, or changed
file is detectable without creating a self-referential hash.
