## Product lesson and evidence boundary

This is a successful scientific-selection outcome: the system preserved the
formal work while stopping before prose production once the contribution was
judged application-only. It is not evidence that the system can yet generate a
strong paper, lower researcher effort, deliver provider-independent behavior,
or establish multi-agent superiority. The continuation was interactive and
researcher-approved, not a fresh blind model evaluation.

The next research-facing improvement should therefore be small and targeted:
make nondegeneracy, rival-mechanism, and contribution/absorption checks happen
earlier in a new case. Those checks should inform the first small
scholar-derived `ResearchMove` batch; they do not justify reopening this one.
