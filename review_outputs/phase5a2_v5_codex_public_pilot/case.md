## Research seed

An infinite sequence of short-lived buyers chooses between two long-lived
sellers. In each period a seller privately observes whether its service
capacity is high or low. A high-capacity seller may pay cost `k` for a truthful
public certificate. A certificate remains visible until one buyer trades with
that seller, at which point it is consumed. An uncertified seller may be high
or low. Before choosing, a buyer can pay search cost `c` to inspect one
uncertified seller's current capacity, can buy without inspecting, or can exit.
A successful service gives the buyer value one; sellers earn a margin from a
successful trade and discount future profits.
